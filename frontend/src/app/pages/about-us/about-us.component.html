WHAT IS NutriTrack

NutriTrack aims to offer a comprehensive platform where individuals can monitor their dietary habits and nutritional intake. Users can log their meals, track their calorie consumption and analyze their intake of various nutrients. They can set dietary goals and monitor their progress over time to ensure they meet their nutritional needs.
